About Me

My name is Briana Hoangan Nguyen and I'm currently a student at New England College with a graphic design major. I was born in January 30, 2002 at Nashua, New Hampshire. I enjoy listening to music, exercising, reading manga, and watching anime.
I came from a Vietnamese Christian family. My family attends church services on the weekends: Vietnamese bible services on Saturday evenings and English bible services on Sunday mornings.
I'm a graphic designer from New Hampshire and specialize in illustrating brands, packaging, prints, and advertising. I developed a strong interest in art during my whole 12 year education in the Nashua School District. My developed a talent in graphic design after taking a NTC (Nashua Technology Center) program, Design and Visual Communications, at Nashua High School North.
I'm currently attending New England College with only one year left until I earn my Bachelor of Fine Arts degree and graduate college
