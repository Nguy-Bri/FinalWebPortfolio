Game Avatar Sprite — Pixel App (2020):
For this project, I used Adobe Illustrator created a concept sketch of myself as a game character (Water Fairy Princess), choose a limited color palette for my character, and then translate your concept sketch into a 32 by 32 pixel sprite with the same design constraints used by programmers of the Nintendo Entertainment System.
I made it in PixelApp, a simple web-based tool for creating pixel art, game sprites, and animated GIFs.

A-Frame (2023): Click here to see the 3D version of my game sprite using A-Frame and Glitch.
