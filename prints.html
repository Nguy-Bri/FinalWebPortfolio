Photoshop Processing — Adobe Photoshop (2020): For this project, I picked 2 or 3 images to use in the background and used clipping masks in Adobe Photoshop to insert sections of some images into the text. Each image has phrases or lyrics of the songs that I like (most of them are from church services).

When You Let It Shine - This image shows a shinning full moon in the night sky. The lyrics are from the song, Make It Shine, from Victorious.
Lets Give Our Thanks - The phrase and the images represents Thanksgiving theme (Turkeys and pumpkins)
I Worship You Forevermore - These lyrics are one of the songs used in church services.
Will You Meet Me Here Again - These lyrics are one of the songs used in church services. I used the clipping masks to apply the yellow bird on the upper right.
Praise The Father Praise The Sun - I used the clipping masks for the text and owl. This lyrics are also the title of the songs used in church services (Note: The last word in the lyrics, "sun", is a typo. It’s supposed to be "son").

Indesign Booklet — The Friendship Guide For Your Dog (2020): This project involves creating a booklet in Adobe InDesign about a topic I chose, dogs. The topic for my booklet involves good tips on creating a strong, confortable friendship with pet dogs. The pictures I picked for my booklet feature my older sister's dog, Gabe, and other dogs in daycare.
(Note: Some of the photos I used in the booklet were not taken by me, they belong to their rightful owners.)
